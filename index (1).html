It's funny how some things start
completely by accident.

02

03

04

One conversation turned into hours

05

Somewhere between that first late night

06

and honestly, that's one of the most
rare, beautiful things about you.

07

The more I get to know you,
the more I realise
you're something I can't quite put into words.

08

Some stories start loud.
Ours started quietly
with one late-night conversation —
and somehow it became my favourite story.

I love you, Anagha. ❤️
