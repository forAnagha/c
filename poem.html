A Poem for Anagha

written with everything I have
🌸
I

Anagha isn't the type who tries to stand out —
she just naturally does.

Different from everyone else
in the best way possible.

II

I don't think I've ever seen her be wrong,
and honestly I don't even know how…
Sometimes it feels like she isn't even real.

But at the same time
she's the most real person I've ever met.

III

She can't fake anything.
Her smile isn't forced.
Her vibes are never pretend.

There's something so pure about her —
the way she talks,
the way she cares,
the way she simply exists.

IV

Still, she tries to hide her pain —
to stay strong all the time,
like she doesn't want anyone to worry.

She carries more than she shows,
and that quiet strength
is one of the most beautiful things about her.

V

She's like an angel,
not because she's perfect —
though sometimes it honestly feels like she is —

but because her heart is.

VI

She's the most beautiful woman in my life.
Not just for how she looks,
but for who she is inside.

And I'm so grateful
that somehow the universe
decided I deserved to know her.

VII

She feels unreal sometimes…
but one thing I know for certain —
she could never be fake.

She is real, and she is mine,
and I will never stop being amazed by that.

I love you, Anagha. ❤️
I tried my best with words…
but I know no words could ever fully describe you.
